Buddy's Breakfast Spaghetti

A colorful spaghetti breakfast for the rambunctious sweet tooth!

If you've seen the movie "Elf", you'd know that elves love their sugar. They always try to
stick to the four main food groups: candy, candy canes, candy corn, and syrup.
This recipe covers two of the four!

If you haven't seen the movie, then you're in for a treat (literally). Recreate this memorable
scene and maybe you'll be bouncing with joy and energy just like Buddy!

Ingredients:

  • 16 ounces - Spaghetti noodles
  • 2 cups - Mini marshmallows
  • 10 ounces - M&M's (plain or peanut)
  • 4 ounces - Rainbow sprinkles
  • 1 Bottle - Chocolate syrup
  • 1 Bottle - Maple syrup
  • 1 piece - Chocolate fudge Pop Tart
    **Note that most of the toppings will be added to your liking

Recipe Instructions:

  1. Boil spaghetti according to the instructions on the package or box, about 10 minutes.
    (If you throw a noodle at the wall and it sticks, 'tis cooked to perfection.)
  2. When the pasta is close to being done, put the chocolate fudge Pop Tart in a toaster.
    After it's toasted, set aside for later.
  3. Drain the spaghetti noodles in a collander and run cold water over the noodles to prevent over-cooking.
  4. Place desired amount of noodles on a clean dinner plate.
  5. Add your toppings: garnish your pasta with M&M's, rainbow sprinkles, and mini marshmallows.
    Measure with love; the more the merrier (and sweeter)!
  6. Drizzle (or drench) your spaghetti with the chocolate and maple syrup.
    Like it really sweet? Squeeze the bottles until empty.
  7. Break the Pop Tart into two pieces and place along side your spaghetti. Consider this your toast!
